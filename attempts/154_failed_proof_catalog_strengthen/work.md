## Strengthening (publishable form)

### Title (revised)
*"A Catalog of Failed RH Proofs: A 6-Category Framework for Critical Reading"*

### Abstract (revised)
The Riemann Hypothesis (RH) has attracted numerous proof attempts over 167 years (since Riemann himself acknowledged "fleeting futile attempts" in 1859). The vast majority failed. We present a 6-category framework for systematic critical reading: (A) trivial circularity, (B) reference circularity, (C) identity transplant, (D) multi-valued inversion, (E) self-acknowledged speculation, (F) abstraction-concrete gap. Each category identifies a distinct failure mode, illustrated with paper-direct examples (Atiyah 2018, de Branges 1986+, BBM 2017 hypothetical, Lagarias §8 (1) hypothetical). The framework serves as a systematic checklist for proof-writers and reviewers.
